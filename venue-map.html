Interactive Venue Maps

First prototyped in my free time to explore the feasibility of using WebGL to render visually stunning and interactive venue maps; It soon became my primary focus at Fanxchange.

Since it's first launch, the library of maps available has grown to cover all major sports across North America and continues to expand.

Now you can find these interactive maps on fanxchange.com, the Fanxchange iOS App, and across a growing list of partners, including groupon.com.
